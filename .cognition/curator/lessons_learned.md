# Lessons Learned

> Specific, actionable lessons captured after each iteration, incident, release, or experiment.

A lesson is not "we did the thing". A lesson is **what we learned that we did not know before** and what we will do differently.

## How to Write a Lesson

Each lesson has:

* **Date**
* **Context** — what was happening
* **What happened** — the actual outcome
* **What we expected** — and how it differed
* **Why** — root cause / mechanism
* **What we will do differently** — concrete change
* **Related** — links to ADRs, issues, retrospectives
