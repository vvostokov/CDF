# Backlog

> An ordered list of work items not yet scheduled into an iteration. Ordered by value × risk ÷ effort, with ties broken by dependencies.

## How to Read This

* The **top** of the list is the most valuable, lowest-risk, lowest-effort unstarted work.
* Items move **down** as they are scheduled, worked on, or deprioritized.
* Each item should reference an issue or ADR.
